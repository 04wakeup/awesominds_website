Background

Greetings, We Are Adam, Walker and James and together we are team Awesominds. Awesominds is a web-based learning app that allows Camosun Instructors to add their content and make it available to their students in the form of games. The Awesominds Project has been worked on by two previous capstone groups. For this phase of the project we will be looking at improving some of the instructor menus such as adding start dates and end dates to chapters, also game changes like adding a stop feature for the game.

Project Sponsor

Marty Donatelli is the mastermind behind this project. Marty is a Psychology instructor at Camosun College’s Lansdowne location. Marty came up with this idea because he wanted to make an application that would help his students learn in a more fun and engaging way.

Explorer AwesomindsII

Awesominds is a web-based learning app that allows instructors to present course content in a fun and engaging way. Student can choose from several different tasks and games to study course material. Instructors can add and altar courses, chapters, students, and more.

Select Mode

Select from 4 modes to learn.

Rate Question

Answer a question and rate how hard you found it to provide analytics.

Slide Card

Test your memory with slide cards.

Just Drills

Answer question after question in just drills.

Game Show

Take part in a multi-round game show with several different challenges against AI opponents.

Keep Choosing

Choose until you get it right, the points awarded go down for each wrong answer.

One Crack

One chance to choose the right answer, if you’re wrong you missed your chance and move on to the next question.

One Crack Time Bonus

One crack but with a timer added, bonus points are awarded for each second remaining

Choose 1, 2, or 3

Choose up to 3 answers to submit, more answers mean you’re more likely to get it right, but are awarded less points

Big Money

3 chances to get the right answer, with a potential huge point reward. If you don’t get it on the first try, you will lose points.
